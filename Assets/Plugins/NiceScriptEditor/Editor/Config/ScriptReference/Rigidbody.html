OnCollisionEnter:OnCollisionEnter is called when this collider/rigidbody has begun touching another rigidbody/collider. OnCollisionExit:OnCollisionExit is called when this collider/rigidbody has stopped touching another rigidbody/collider. OnCollisionStay:OnCollisionStay is called once per frame for every collider/rigidbody that is touching rigidbody/collider.
